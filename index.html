UU Estrie Tools

A small set of helpers for running things at the congregation. Pick a tool below. Nothing here requires a login.

Service Prep

For the week's service leader: answer a few questions and generate the filled-in Service Script and Order of Service as PDFs. Saves progress automatically.

Expense Reimbursement

Submit a receipt or invoice to be reimbursed for a church expense. Attach the receipt and send it to the treasurer.

Invoice Generator

Create a simple invoice from the church — add line items and prices, then print or save it as a PDF to send.
